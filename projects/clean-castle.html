Clean Castle

Clean Castle is a Sokoban-inspired mobile puzzle game that reimagines the classic formula with fresh mechanics like traps, stairs, locks, and dragon eggs, all designed to challenge players with greater depth and variety.

As the Level Designer, I crafted over 200 unique levels using the game's custom-built level editor. These ranged from accessible introductory puzzles to complex, high-difficulty challenges that tested even the most experienced players. My focus was on pacing, difficulty curves, and introducing mechanics in a way that felt both intuitive and rewarding.

Tools: Unity

Year: 2022
