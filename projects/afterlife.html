Afterlife: The Judgement

This game was a project developed for the discipline "Quick Development Tools for Games" at Unisinos in 2016.

In "Afterlife: The Judgment," each player controls a person's soul at the moment of their final judgment. The objective is for players to keep their soul on one of the 5 lower platforms for a predetermined time, while 5 upper platforms attempt to push their soul towards hell. The soul that falls first goes to hell, while the other goes to paradise.

Tools: Unity 3D, Visual Studio 2015, Adobe Photoshop

Programming Language: C#

My Role: As **Game Designer**, I focused on the core mechanics and player experience. As **Tech Artist**, I implemented visual effects and optimized assets for performance.
