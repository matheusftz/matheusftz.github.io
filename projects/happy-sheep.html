Happy Sheep

"Happy Sheep" aimed to be a behavioral animation development experience, made for the disciplines of Computer Animation and Artificial Intelligence at Unisinos in 2017. The game was inspired by other games like The Sims and Tamagotchi. If the player does not interact for some time, an AI takes the place of the player (represented by the robot cursor).

Tools: Unity, Blender, Photoshop

Programming Language: C#
