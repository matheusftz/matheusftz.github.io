Project L.U.A.

"Project L.U.A." is a project made for Games Workshop discipline at Unisinos in 2017.

We are at the future and a large alien war has devastated several planets, launched asteroids and wreckage towards Earth. Several leaders of Earth's nations came together to create Project L.U.A. (Lunar Ultimate Aid) as an attempt to protect our planet from the various threats that were to come.

In Project L.U.A, you have control of several new defensive and offensive technologies that have been implemented on a lunar base to defend Earth and the Moon itself.

Tools: Unity 3D, Visual Studio 2015, Adobe Maya and Adobe Photoshop

Programming Language: C#
