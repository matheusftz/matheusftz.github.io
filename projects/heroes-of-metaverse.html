Heroes of Metaverse

Heroes of Metaverse is a Brazilian-developed, roguelike action RPG for Android. Players explore procedurally generated worlds, battle monsters and bosses, and aim to survive as long as possible. Each run resets progress on death, but allows you to keep earned gear, encouraging strategic builds and replayability. The game features real-time combat, customizable heroes, and a wide variety of equipment to master.

As the Game Designer, I was responsible for implementing and balancing core gameplay features, including new enemies, power-ups, and weapons, as well as refining combat systems and progression curves to enhance player engagement.

Tools: Unity, ClickUp, Slack, Excel, Qase

Year: 2022
