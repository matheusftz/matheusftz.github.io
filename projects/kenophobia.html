Kenophobia

"Kenophobia" is a project made for Alternative Platforms discipline at Unisinos in 2018. The Alpha space station was attacked by a mysterious and aggressive life form that disabled the life support systems, taking the atmosphere out of the station, the player is a passenger of the Alpha station who was a survivor in the attack. He must search the station for some escape ship while he has to monitor his oxygen and escape from alien creature. Controls: The buttons on the Arduino represent the character's feet and the player needs to switch the clicks to simulate each character's step to move around the map.

Tools: Unity

Programming Language: C#

Year: 2018
