Afterlife: The Judgement

This game is a project made for the discipline Quick Development Tools for Games at Unisinos in 2016.

In the game "Afterlife: The Judgment", each player controls a person's soul at the time of their final judgment. The objective of the players is to make their soul stay on the 5 platforms down for the time determined by their pre-judgment, while 5 platforms from above try to push their soul to hell. The soul that falls first goes to hell while the other goes to paradise.

Tools: Unity 3D, Visual Studio 2015 and Adobe Photoshop

Programming Language: C#
